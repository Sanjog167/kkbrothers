About Kabin International

Kabin International Pvt. Ltd. is a one the youngest and fastest growing brand and also is an authorized distributor of Sonalika Tractors for Bagmati and Koshi Zone of Nepal. With a focused approach to serve the automobile community, Kabin International have become one of the leading brand and gained the trust of more than 10 Lakh customers through its innovative range of new tractors and agri solutions. When it comes to customer satisfaction, we don't hesitate to go above and beyond to help you. We are focused on meeting all your restoration needs while offering value and quality with every tractor part we deliver.
