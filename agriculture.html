About New P. R. Suppliers

Nepal has an excellent potential in agricultural production; this is a sector which can add value both within and outside the country. Nepalese agricultural products such as spices, herbs, grains, and certain fruits and vegetables are unmatched in quality. Furthermore, there are specific products that are unique to Nepal’s geography and climate. The group intends to encourage the scalability of agricultural production all over the country by creating strong demand links in both domestic and international markets. The dream is to have the world recognize Nepalese agricultural products for their distinct quality. All the products will be 100% organic.

We are a model organic farms practice sustainable agriculture in Nepal and a verified distributor of Sonalika Agriculture Tools, utilizing the modern techniques of farming in Nepal and produces verity of organic foods. We have been offering comprehensive guide on farming, specific agriculture techniques and management, quality control & sustainability solutions for businesses and for those who want to have their career in Agriculture Industry since 2008 AD. We never use chemical fertilizer and pesticides, birds and animals are feeding grains, grass and grazing on natural pasture.
