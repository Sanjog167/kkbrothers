About KK Brothers

"WE ARE PROUD TO DELIVER PROJECTS THAT SHAPE OUR COMMUNITIES AND STRENGTHEN OUR NATION'S INFRASTRUCTURE."

KK Brothers is a leading well-known and well-established "A" class company in Nepal having over 25 years of experience in all types of construction and infrastructure development works. The Company was founded by Late Hom bahadur shrestha in 2000 A.D. KK Brothers has five operating companies, 'HM Construction (P) Ltd.', 'Kabin International (P) Ltd. ' , 'Sunsari Saving & Credit Co-operative Ltd.' , 'H.M Remitts (P) Ltd.', 'Nepal International Hospitality (P) Ltd.' in five business sectors: construction, automobiles, finance, remittance and hospitality respectively.


KK Brothers has been very sincere about on time delivery, performance, professional standards and quality since its establishment. Our clients have appreciated the highly competitive costs, delivery, quality and efficiency of our construction works. We believe that the company has progressed significantly since its humble beginnings and our strategy is to continue to pursue our growth. In every aspect of its operations, the company's approach reflects a deep concern for the environment and an awareness of the importance in maintaining balance between the needs of people and society and the preservation of natural resources. KK Brothers will continue to maintain this perspective as it contributes to the prosperity of the nation through its involvement in public and private sector projects.
